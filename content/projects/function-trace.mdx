<Heading typo="h2">Available Features</Heading>
<Paragraph>
    function-trace is a minimal, zero-dependency TypeScript utility that gives you deep visibility into your application's real performance behavior with almost zero effort.
</Paragraph>
<ul>
    <li>One-line instrumentation for any sync or async function</li>
    <li>Automatic tracking of execution time, call count & error rate</li>
    <li>Configurable thresholds with warning/error logging</li>
    <li>Real-time statistics ready for monitoring & alerting</li>
    <li>Works seamlessly across Node.js, browsers, serverless & edge</li>
</ul>

    <li>Native JavaScript (Performance API)</li>
    <li>No external dependencies</li>
</ol>
