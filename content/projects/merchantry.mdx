<Heading typo="h2">Available Features</Heading>
<Paragraph>
    `Merchantry` is a comprehensive platform designed to facilitate the buying
    and selling of used products. It offers a robust set of features aimed at
    providing a seamless user experience for both buyers and sellers. Key
    features include:
</Paragraph>
<ul>
    <li>
        Easy user/seller account creation, enabling quick and straightforward
        registration.
    </li>
    <li>Ability to add, buy, and manage products efficiently.</li>
    <li>An intuitive `admin` dashboard for better management and oversight.</li>
    <li>Multiple payment options to ensure smooth transactions.</li>
    <li>Strong authentication options to secure user accounts.</li>
</ul>


<Heading typo="h2">Technology Used</Heading>
<Paragraph>
    `Merchantry` leverages a modern technology stack to deliver a powerful and
    scalable application. The technologies used include:
</Paragraph>
<ol>
    <li>`ReactJS` and react-router for a dynamic and responsive front-end.</li>
    <li>
        `MongoDB` and `Firebase` for robust data storage and real-time
        capabilities.
    </li>
    <li>`TailwindCSS` & Daisy UI for sleek and customizable styling.</li>
    <li>`ExpressJS` for a reliable and efficient `backend`.</li>
</ol>

<Paragraph>
    In summary, `Merchantry` is an ideal solution for individuals looking to buy
    or sell used products. It combines ease of use, robust functionality, and a
    modern technology stack to ensure a seamless and secure experience for all
    users.
</Paragraph>
